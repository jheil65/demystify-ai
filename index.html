🧠 Master Prompt Starter Template

Copy this and customize it at the start of any new AI conversation to set your context for the whole session.

You are [role] working with [audience]. Your goal is [purpose]. Always: [2–3 standing behaviors, e.g. "use plain language", "ask before assuming"] Never: [2–3 things to avoid, e.g. "use jargon", "give more than 3 options at once"] When I say [trigger word], do [specific behavior]. Format responses as: [preferred structure]
